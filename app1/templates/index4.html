Delicious Recipe Tutorial

Recipe Title

Ingredients

  • 2 cups watermelon puree (seedless)
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream (softened)
  • Plastic wrap or parchment paper

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Prepare the Watermelon Layer: Cut a seedless watermelon into chunks and blend them until smooth to get about 2 cups of watermelon puree. Strain the watermelon puree to remove any excess water. In a mixing bowl, combine the watermelon puree, powdered sugar, and lemon juice. Adjust the sweetness according to your preference. Mix well until the sugar is dissolved.

  2. Step 2:Spread the Watermelon Layer: Line a rectangular baking tray or a flat container with plastic wrap or parchment paper, leaving some overhang on the sides to help with rolling later. Pour the watermelon mixture into the lined tray, spreading it evenly to form a thin layer.

  3. Step 3:Freeze the Watermelon Layer: Place the tray in the freezer and let the watermelon layer freeze until it's firm but not completely solid. This should take about 1 to 2 hours.

  4. Step 4:Add the Ice Cream Layer: Once the watermelon layer is semi-frozen, remove the tray from the freezer. Soften the vanilla ice cream by letting it sit at room temperature for a few minutes. You want it to be soft enough to spread but not melted. Spread the softened ice cream evenly over the partially frozen watermelon layer.

  5. Step 5:Rolling the Ice Cream Roll: Carefully start rolling the dessert from one end, using the plastic wrap or parchment paper to help lift and guide the roll. Roll it tightly but gently, forming a log shape. Wrap the entire roll with the plastic wrap or parchment paper and place it back in the freezer.

  6. Step 6:Final Freeze: Allow the Watermelon Ice Cream Roll to freeze completely, preferably for several hours or overnight. This will ensure that it's firm enough to slice.
